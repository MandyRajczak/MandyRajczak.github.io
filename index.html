Artist Statement

Hello. I’m Mandy Rajczak. A while ago, an illness impacted the way I see and interact with the everyday world. I wanted to paint things just as I saw them. How incredibly beautiful everyday things were. Soon enough though the style didn’t appeal to me. Pops of color were much more. The world is full of hidden colors, where we wouldn’t think to put them. Seeing things the way they are will always be hard. Portraits and the human body are subjects that are easily messed up and those that can be saved. The human body can look right and wrong at the same time. Color and the human body are two things that interest me the most.

Please enjoy my art. :)
